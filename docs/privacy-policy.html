Privacy Policy

Rooted – Your Bible Note-Taking Companion

Overview

Rooted is a Bible note-taking app designed to help you reflect on Scripture without distractions. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Rooted does not:

  • Collect or store any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Require you to create an account or log in
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Upload your notes to any server

Data Stored on Your Device

All your data stays on your device. Rooted stores the following locally using on-device storage (SQLite and AsyncStorage):

  • Notes – Your note titles, content, and event names
  • Settings – Your preferred Bible translation, Focus Mode preference, and accountability reminder intervals
  • Cached Bible verses – Verse text is cached locally so you can access it offline
  • Notification data – Scheduled accountability reminder information

This data is never transmitted off your device. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Network Requests

Rooted makes network requests solely to fetch Bible verse text when you tap on a verse reference. These requests are made to:

  • bible.helloao.org – for most Bible translations
  • bolls.life – for the New King James Version (NKJV)

These requests contain only the book, chapter, and verse numbers needed to retrieve the text. No personal or identifying information is included in these requests.

Notifications

Rooted may send local push notifications as accountability reminders if you enable this feature. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

Children's Privacy

Rooted does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy
